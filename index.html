Background

Business start small and with elementary structures. As they grow, business owners fail to realize the risk of non-compliance with regulatory requirements. The tax authorities are usually the ones that come knocking first. When this happens, uncompliant businesses are slammed with penalties and interest on unremitted statutory payments. Surprisingly, compliance is not expensive as organization are not required have to have all the compliance resources in-house.

The employees of non-compliant entities also suffer in the process. They have nothing to fall back to later in life when they are no longer gainfully employed as they do not contribute to at the minimum the state pension scheme (NSSF). They are also not covered for work injuries. Our service offering encompasses other service that benefit employees including allowing them to join savings and credit societies (Saccos). Buy Future Associates has set out to be the intermediary that serves both employees and businesses to create better value for all.
